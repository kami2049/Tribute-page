Annie Easley

Computer Scientist and Mathematician

Anne Easley was one of the first four African American employees to work for NASA when it was created in 1958.She was on the front line of space research and missions that started with the launch of John Glenn into orbit in 1962.Easley also worked on nuclear-powered rocket systems like the Centaur high-energy booster rocket.
